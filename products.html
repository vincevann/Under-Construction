Products We Offer

We offer carefully selected insurance plans that provide powerful coverage and flexibility. Our focus is on real protection for real risks-plans that do more than sit on paper. Here's a breakdown of what we offer:

  • Critical Illness Insurance: Helps cover costs from serious diagnoses like heart attack, stroke, or cancer. Many plans come with a lump sum payout and a cash-back option if unused.
  • Disability Insurance: Protects your income if you're unable to work due to injury or illness. Perfect for new hires, maternity coverage, or anyone without many sick days.
  • Accident & Hospital Plans: Covers unexpected events-from falls to hospital stays. Benefits pay out based on the type of treatment, not just your medical bills.
  • Long-Term Care (LTC): Helps you plan for aging or chronic illness care-so your family isn't left to carry the burden alone.
  • Life Insurance: Protects your loved ones financially and can be bundled with savings or investment features. Many of our plans offer cash-back if unused after 25 years.

We also offer bundled plans with Critical Illness + Life coverage that provide multiple layers of protection and long-term value. If you want to compare your current plan to ours, we'll help you do a benefit vs. cost analysis-side by side, apples to apples.
